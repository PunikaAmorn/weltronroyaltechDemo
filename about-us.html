Weltron Royal Tech is the leader in enterprise printing solutions for the industrial market placeand distribution supply chain, providing network integrated multi-technologyprinters to businesses and private individuals worldwide. Weltron Royal Tech is the acknowledged foremost supplier of line matrix printers and an innovator in continuous forms of Labels, Sticker, Thermal Transfer Ribbons, laser printers and thermal bar code scanners and printers. Weltron Royal Tech has also become an established leader in pioneering printing technologies, including radio frequency identification (RFID) printing, bar code compliance and networked printer management. Based in Thailand, with prospects of expanding Worldwide, Weltron Royal Tech employees are represented all over Thailand. In addition to its corporate offices in Bangkok Thailand, Weltron Royal Tech also has certified partners in America, Europe, Singapore, China, India, Korea and Australia. Weltron Royal Tech technology is sold under private label to the major computer systems providers and is available under the Weltron Royal Tech brand name through full-service distributors and value-added resellers.

Leadership by Design For more than 20 years, Weltron Royal Tech has designed and delivered the broadest range of industrial printing technologies and solutions for global enterprise. From market leading Labels, Sticker, Ribbons, line matrix printers and the industry’s first commercially available UHF RFID printers to high-performance thermal bar code label printers and continuous forms of laser printers, Weltron Royal Tech products addresses the need of customers worldwide. Our comprehensive capabilities, experience, and leadership in numerous industries has produced a wealth of proven printing solutions. These solutions have helped organizations increase operational efficiencies, reduce costs, improve supply chain visibility and manage printing application requirements across the entire enterprise and around the globe.
